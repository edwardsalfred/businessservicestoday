A short history.

The Whitener Group opened on Westpark Drive in 2015 to serve Texas small-business owners the old-fashioned way: pick up the phone, hand over a binder, answer questions in person. We started with five filings in our first year. Eleven years later we've completed more than twelve hundred, and we still work from the same office suite in southwest Houston.

What we believe.

  • A physical binder is better than a PDF. Clients keep them. Bankers trust them. Partners notice them.
  • The state filing fee should never be rolled into a service fee. Transparency builds trust.
  • Veterans deserve the SB 938 pathway handled end-to-end. Not mentioned in a blog post.
  • Formation is step one. Credit, funding, bonds, and benefits come after — which is why we built an ecosystem.
  • If we file it, we pick up the phone when the IRS calls. That's what "backed" means.

Where we fit.

We don't try to be ZenBusiness. They ship great software to fifty states. We ship a binder to one state — Texas — and we know the Secretary of State forms, the Comptroller's quirks, and the SB 938 veteran pathway the way only a local firm can. If you want a Wix-style experience, they're better. If you want a human in Houston who knows your EIN by memory, we're better.
