Executive summary

The Texas LLC formation niche is dominated by five national SaaS players with near-identical design languages. Business Services Today has three structural advantages none of them can copy.

BST's Weebly-era site buries its biggest strengths (the physical Corporate Identity Binder, Houston family-run service, and veteran SB 938 specialization) under a flat list of services and dated templates. The rebuild positions BST as the editorial, Texas-first alternative to ZenBusiness and LegalZoom: warm paper tones, navy + gold, serif headlines, and photography of the actual binder. The playbook that follows is grounded in scoring the top ten names in the niche and deep-profiling the top five.

Patterns of the top 10%

Five moves every winning site makes that the bottom of the pack skips.

  • Name the outcome, not the service. Every winner leads with transformation, "Start your business with confidence", "We make it easy", not "LLC filing services." BST's current site leads with the mechanic.
  • Pricing visible, not hidden behind a form. All five top sites show tier pricing on the homepage or one click in. BST buries $558 / $750 in a mid-page checkout button, a conversion killer.
  • Show social proof where friction spikes. Star ratings and review counts appear in the nav, near CTAs, and inside pricing cards, not only in a testimonial band at the bottom.
  • Three pillars, never more. Start / Protect / Grow. Form / File / Forward. Top sites reduce complexity to exactly three ideas. BST currently lists 10+ services flat, with no hierarchy.
  • Prove humanity with faces and names. Northwest names its 200 Corporate Guides. LegalZoom photographs real entrepreneurs. BST's untapped moats of family-run, Houston, and physical binder live or die on showing actual humans and actual product.

Recommended design direction

How the rebuild turns BST's moats into visual language.

Color

Deep navy (#0B2545) for authority, warm gold (#C9962B) for the premium binder motif, burnt orange (#C45D3E) for CTAs, served on a warm paper background (#F6F2EC). Deliberately the opposite of competitor teal-and-white.

Typography

Fraunces (variable serif) for headings: editorial authority, pairs with the physical binder premium positioning. Inter for body: strong legibility at small sizes. A combination none of the top five use.

Fraunces · headings Inter · body

Architecture

Six public pages (Home, LLC, Veteran, Grants, About, Contact) anchored by a three-pillar value prop (Filed · Bound · Backed). Pricing visible above the fold. Veteran SB 938 pathway gets its own band and dedicated page.

Motion

GSAP + ScrollTrigger. Fade + slide on every section entrance, parallax on the binder hero, hover-lift on pricing cards. prefers-reduced-motion respected throughout. Cinematic but not gimmicky.
