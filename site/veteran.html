How SB 938 works

Three requirements. One form. We coordinate all of it.

01

100% Veteran-Owned

Every owner on the filing must be an honorably discharged veteran of the U.S. Armed Services.

02

DD-214 on File

Discharge papers confirm honorable status. We coordinate the Veteran Verification Letter with the Texas Veterans Commission.

03

New Entity

SB 938 applies to LLCs formed on or after January 1, 2022, not conversions of existing entities.
