## 1. Company & Positioning

The Whitener Group LLC, operating as **Business Services Today**, is a Houston, TX based business services firm. Unlike national SaaS filing platforms, BST delivers a **physical Corporate Identity Binder** (branded 3-ring binder containing Secretary of State filings, EIN, Texas Comptroller docs, operating agreements, compliance meeting docs, W-9s, etc.) — a distinctive, tangible deliverable.

BST serves three core audiences:
1. **Texas small business owners** forming LLCs / C-Corps
2. **Honorably discharged veterans** leveraging Texas SB 938 (free LLC formation for veteran-owned businesses until 5th anniversary)
3. **Entrepreneurs / nonprofit founders** looking for grant funding

BST is part of a sister-company ecosystem covering business credit, funding, credit training, worksite benefits, and surety bonds:
- Lone Star Neighborhood Development LLC
- TrueBusinessCredit.com
- TrueBusinessFunding.com
- CreditTrainingAcademy.com
- TexasWorksiteBenefits.com
- TheBondDepot.net

## 9. Brand Color Rationale

BST's existing site has no strong palette (Weebly default blues and grays). The proposed palette is a **deliberate upgrade** grounded in:

- **Deep Navy** — reads as trust, legal, established (competitors LegalZoom, Northwest all lean blue)
- **Warm Gold** — evokes the physical binder + premium deliverable + Texas sun
- **Burnt Orange** — Texas warmth, UT-associable, strong CTA color
- **Warm paper ink-on-cream** — editorial feel that sets BST apart from clinical SaaS competitors

## 10. Font Rationale

- **Fraunces** (serif, variable) — editorial authority, reads as "established firm" rather than "startup". Pairs beautifully with gold accent and Texas heritage feel.
- **Inter** — modern sans-serif, clean body copy, strong legibility at small sizes, free via Google Fonts.
